Hi. My name is Joel and I have three young daughters. Sometimes it's hard to balance it all.

In this survival guide, I will teach you my proven secrets how to do it.

Looking at my typical day, here are some (in no particular order), items I might be responsible for:

  • Making dinner for the family.
  • Getting the girls dressed in the morning and making them breakfast.
  • Doing a short grocery run after work.

One way to simplify life is to have a few easy meals you can cook practically instantly. Of course, I'd still like them to be healthy though, not just mac 'n cheese. Now, some dining plans like Blue Apron say "easy to cook in just 15 minutes" but really it's 5 minutes to open the box and remove all the packaging waste, another half hour to chop everything super tiny, and then another 15 to cook. It's another 30 minutes to Google what I should do with all that waste, especially the frozen "ice" packets, which say "DO NOT PUT DOWN DRAIN" (exactly what I wanted to do). 30 minutes later and 15 Chrome tabs open learning all about waste and toxins, the "ice" packets are in the trash, surely leaking out into a horrible mess that the trash man is going to drip all over my driveway the next day...

So yeah, not like that.
